Golf Every Day

Golf is the chess of the sports world.

Here is why you should try

  • Golf is not a team sport, so you can improve on your own.
  • Golf can be played with friends, strangers, or alone.
  • Golf is something that takes constant work.

How to get started

  1. Go to a local Top Golf to see if it's for you. They will provide with clubs and there are tons of low skill games to make it interesting for pure beginners.
  2. Buy a cheap used set. You don't need much to get started.
  3. Spend some time at Putt-Putt or the putting green at your local course. It's normally free to go practice your putting at a local course.
